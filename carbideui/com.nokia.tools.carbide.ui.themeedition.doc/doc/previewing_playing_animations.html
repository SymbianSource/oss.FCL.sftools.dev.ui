Previewing and Playing Animations

  • In the Editor, click the animated component.
  • Right-click the component and select Edit/Animate selected elements.
  • The Animation editor opens.
  • To play the animation, click the green play button.
  • You see a preview of the animation in the preview section of the Entity graphics editor view.
  • To pause the animation, click the yellow pause button to pause the animation.
  • To continue playing the animation, click the green play button.
  • To stop the animation, click the red stop button.
  • The animation stops. The first frame of the animation is displayed in the preview section.
